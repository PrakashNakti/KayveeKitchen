About Kayvee Kitchens

Interiors built around
how you actually live

For nearly two decades, Kayvee Kitchens has been designing and fitting modular kitchens, wardrobes and complete interiors for homes and offices across Mumbai — from Malad to the wider suburbs.

We start with a measured drawing of your space, not a catalogue. Every cabinet, shutter and worktop is planned for your storage, your appliances and your routine, then manufactured and installed by our own team — so the kitchen you approve on paper is the one you get fitted at home, supported by warranty on materials and hardware.

What We Do

Our Services

Five disciplines, one team — so your kitchen, storage and interiors are designed to work together, not as separate jobs.

01

Modular Kitchen Design

Layouts planned around your cooking habits — L-shape, parallel or island — with shutters, finishes and hardware to match.

02

Interior Design

Complete home and office interiors — living rooms, false ceilings, lighting and finishes designed as one cohesive space.

03

Kitchen Renovation

Replacing an old kitchen without starting from zero — we work around existing plumbing, gas lines and electricals.

04

Wardrobes & Storage

Sliding, hinged or walk-in wardrobes and storage units, fitted to the centimetre for awkward corners and low ceilings.

05

Custom Furniture

TV units, study tables, crockery units and other made-to-order furniture finished to match your interiors.
